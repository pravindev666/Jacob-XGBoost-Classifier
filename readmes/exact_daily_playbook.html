This is what you do on a day when confidence is above 55%.
1
Read the signal bar. Example: "Bull Put Spread — Execute"
Note down: Strategy · Short strike · Long strike · Net credit · Max loss · Breakeven
2
Open Zerodha Kite → F&O → NIFTY → Options Chain
Set expiry to current week (Thursday expiry). Find the strikes shown in the dashboard.
3
For Bull Put Spread: Place TWO orders simultaneously
Order 1: SELL the short strike PE (e.g. 23700 PE) — Product: MIS or NRML — Qty: 1 lot (50 units)
Order 2: BUY the long strike PE (e.g. 23500 PE) — same expiry — Qty: 1 lot
Net credit = premium received − premium paid = your max profit
4
Immediately log it in Trade Journal page
Fill: Date, Strategy, Confidence %, VIX at entry, Strikes, Lots, Premium collected. Click Save Trade.
5
Set ONE reminder on your phone
"Check Nifty spread P&L" — for every day until expiry. When P&L hits 50% of max profit → exit.
Exit rules — memorise these
Credit spreads: Exit when P&L = 50% of max profit. OR hold to expiry if comfortable.
Option buys: Exit when premium doubles (+50%). Hard stop when premium halves (-50%).
Never: Add more to a losing position. Ever.
